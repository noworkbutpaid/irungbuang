Investor Information: Contract Tax

Image 1: Normal Buy Pressure

With normal buy pressure, token demand is stable, and trading is average. The TwogeCEO tax applies standard fees, ensuring a balanced market and promoting regular trading. This creates a healthy environment for all investors and supports a sustainable ecosystem.

Image 2: High Buy Pressure

When buy pressure is high, token demand increases rapidly, causing potential market volatility. To protect new investors and discourage market manipulation by large investors (whales), the TwogeCEO tax raises transaction fees. This helps maintain market stability and prevents sudden price drops that could harm investors.

As a new investor, understanding the TwogeCEO tax in different market conditions is important. The tax system adjusts fees based on market demand, protecting investors and promoting a secure ecosystem.

During high buy pressure, fees increase to discourage market manipulation. During normal buy pressure, standard fees apply, encouraging regular trading. A portion of the collected fees goes to a dev/marketing wallet, funding buybacks, liquidity injections, and other initiatives that support the project's long-term success.

In short, the TwogeCEO tax system adapts to market conditions, ensuring a stable and secure investment environment.
